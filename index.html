Task Match
Prototype website: Monetising simple to no skill tasks
Executive Summary

TaskMatch is an online platform that connects people looking for help with everyday tasks with users eager to monetize their services. Unlike traditional job sites focused on professional jobs, we cater to simple, task-based jobs that anyone can do.

The Problem

Most traditional job platforms predominantly cater to skilled and white collar professionals, creating a substantial void for individuals seeking assistance with everyday tasks that require little or no skills. This oversight not only limits opportunities for those in need of help but also under-utilizes a vast pool of individuals eager to offer their services for simple, task-based jobs. As a result, many people struggle to find reliable assistance for routine tasks, while potential service providers miss out on opportunities to earn income through accessible, flexible work.

Our Solution

By bridging this gap in the job market, TaskMatch empowers both sides of the equation - those seeking help and those seeking work - to efficiently connect in a simpler and user-friendly environment.

How It Works
  • Job Posting: Users list specific tasks with clear descriptions.
  • Bidding System: Inspired by eBay-style auctions, workers bid on jobs.
  • Timed Auctions: Job listings expire in 5 minutes to 6 hours, ensuring quick responses.
  • Selection Process: Job posters choose from the pool of interested workers.
Key Features
  1. Accessible to all, regardless of educational background
  2. Quick turnaround for immediate task completion
  3. Competitive environment fostering quality service
  4. Monetization of simple, everyday tasks
  5. A large pool of eager service suppliers that generates the sense of urgency.
Target Market
  • Busy professionals needing assistance with errands
  • Homeowners seeking help with chores
  • Individuals looking for flexible, task-based income
Value Proposition

TaskMatch transforms simple, everyday tasks into income opportunities while providing quick, efficient services to those in need.

Conclusion

By bridging the gap between task-givers and task-doers, TaskMatch creates a win-win situation, serving an overlooked market and empowering individuals to earn money through accessible work opportunities.
